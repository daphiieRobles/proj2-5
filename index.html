Some of our specialties

Chiles en nogada

Come and try our chiles en nogada, a traditional dish made of stuffed chiles with a sweet savory filling. Our peppers are drizzled with a decadent walnut sauce, green parsley, and pomegranate seeds, resembling the colors of the Mexican flag. A delicious way to celebrate Mexican Independence Day.

Pozole

Dare to come and try our Pozole Rojo, a rich brothy soup made with pork/chicken, hominy corn, and red chiles. Pile your bowl with topping like shredded cabbage, radishes, cilantro, lime, and avocado!
